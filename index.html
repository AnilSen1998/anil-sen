What is Hit MVP

You know when you come up with a great idea for an app, you get overwhelmed by the sheer amount of work that goes into planning and taking the first steps.

You need to identify user roles, define features, plan out the screens for each feature, estimate the time and cost of building your app, and so on.

You need this information to prioritize your features, set your budget, and communicate your vision to your development team.

But it’s hard to know where to start, and it’s easy to get lost in the details.

Hit MVP is designed to help you plan your app’s features, estimate your project’s timeline, and generate a detailed project proposal in minutes.

The Story Behind Hit MVP

For over 20 years, our team has been at the cutting edge of app development, partnering with innovative startups and Fortune 500 companies alike.

Throughout our journey, one challenge consistently stood out: feature planning and project estimation often became a time-consuming ordeal that frequently missed the mark, frustrating both app owners and developers.

That is why we are building Hit MVP - an automated MVP Planning tool, born from decades of industry wisdom.

This persistent pain point became our call to action. Harnessing our vast experience and insights gleaned from countless projects, we set out to streamline the feature prioritization and estimation process.

By bridging the gap between vision and execution, we’re helping founders launch their products faster and more efficiently.
